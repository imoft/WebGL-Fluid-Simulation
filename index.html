We held a workshop bringing different expertise and perspectives.

The washing machine prototype was the perfect trigger to start our journey. We unpacked features and technologies to bring more business imagination. Participants acted as futurists and entrepreneurs, making the future of laundry feel a bit more tangible. The invisible life of the laundry experience.

From this experience, we curated and organized inisghts and emerged patterns into three different tracks. These tracks have overlaps, so the aim is to establish thought-provoking areas to be further explored.
